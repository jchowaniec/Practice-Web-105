Beginning True Start Computer Gaming Modern Gaming Fun Facts
  • The Game boy was the first console to be played in space.
  • The Play-station 2 was the best-selling console of all time as of 2022
  • In the game “Minecraft”, the overworld is 9,258,235 times larger than the earth, the way the game is able to run all of it is by unloading chunks based on a slider in the menu down to 6 and a maximum of 96
  • The first console to introduce cartridges to gaming was the “Fairchild Channel F” in 1976.
  • The first 3 dimensional game to exist was on the Atari in 1981, known as “Battlezone”.
  • the earliest example of a “video game” was a training program known as “Cathode ray tube Amusement Device” which was created in 1947 as a simulation for firing missiles.
